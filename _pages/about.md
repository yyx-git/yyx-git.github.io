Im Yaxuan Yang, a Master student in the Department of Mechanics at Sichuan University.

My research interests include:
- Typhoon dynamics and controllability
- Reinforcement learning for Typhoon Control
- Ocean–atmosphere interaction and landfall processes

I am currently working on two related topics centered on landfalling typhoons: 
- Landfalling tropical cyclones perturb Earth‘s rotation.
- Reinforcement Learning for Typhoon Intensity Control Before Landfall.
